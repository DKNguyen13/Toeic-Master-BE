3. Cấu trúc câu cơ bản

Cấu trúc thường gặp trong mô tả văn phòng (ETS common patterns):

  • There is / There are + Noun + Preposition: There is a printer on the table.
  • Passive voice for actions: The computer is repaired by IT staff.
  • Imperatives for instructions: Turn on the photocopier before using.
  • Prepositions of place (in, on, at): The filing cabinet is in the corner.
  • Modals for requests: Could you please refill the stapler?

4. Bài tập nhỏ

Bài tập 1: Chọn từ đúng để điền vào chỗ trống (mô phỏng Part 5 TOEIC).

  1. There _____ a new printer in the office.
    A. is | B. are | C. be
  2. The stapler is _____ the desk.
    A. in | B. on | C. at
  3. Documents _____ stored in the filing cabinet.
    A. is | B. are | C. stores
  4. Could you _____ the whiteboard?
    A. write | B. writes | C. writing
  5. The fax machine needs _____ paper.
    A. more | B. many | C. much

Đáp án:

  • 1 – A. is
  • 2 – B. on
  • 3 – B. are
  • 4 – A. write
  • 5 – A. more

Giải thích: 1: Singular noun. 2: Preposition for surface. 3: Passive plural. 4: Modal + base verb. 5: Uncountable noun.

6. Bài tập nâng cao

Bài tập 2: Viết lại câu dùng passive voice hoặc preposition:

  1. The IT team repairs the computer. → (Passive)
  2. The whiteboard is next to the desk. → (Use 'beside')
  3. Turn on the printer. → (Add modal for polite request)

Đáp án mẫu: 1. The computer is repaired by the IT team. 2. The whiteboard is beside the desk. 3. Could you turn on the printer?

7. Bài tập trắc nghiệm nhanh

Chọn đáp án đúng (mô phỏng Part 5):

  1. The photocopier _____ out of order.
    A. is | B. are | C. be
  2. Files are kept _____ the filing cabinet.
    A. in | B. on | C. at
  3. Please _____ the stapler after use.
    A. return | B. returns | C. returning
  4. There _____ chairs in the room.
    A. is | B. are | C. was
  5. The computer screen is _____ bright.
    A. too | B. very | C. much

Đáp án: 1-A, 2-A, 3-A, 4-B, 5-A
