3. Cấu trúc câu cơ bản

Cấu trúc cho lịch trình du lịch (ETS patterns):

  • Future tenses (will, be going to): The flight will depart at 9 AM.
  • Past simple for experiences: I checked in at the hotel yesterday.
  • Time prepositions (at, on, in): Arrival in Tokyo on Monday.
  • Conditionals for delays: If the flight is delayed, we will miss the meeting.

4. Bài tập nhỏ

Bài tập 1: Chọn từ đúng (mô phỏng Part 6).

  1. The _____ time is 10 AM.
    A. arrival | B. departure | C. delay
  2. Make a hotel _____.
    A. itinerary | B. reservation | C. boarding pass
  3. The flight was _____ due to rain.
    A. delayed | B. arrived | C. departed
  4. Check your travel _____.
    A. luggage | B. itinerary | C. check-in

Đáp án: 1-B, 2-B, 3-A, 4-B
